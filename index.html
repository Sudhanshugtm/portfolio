About

I build things with AI as my coding partner. Every project here is a collaboration between my ideas and AI tools helping me implement them. I'm exploring what's possible when you combine human creativity with AI assistance - from ideation to deployment.

Rather than pretending I built everything from scratch, I want to show what modern development looks like: having good ideas, knowing how to work with AI tools, and shipping real solutions that work.
